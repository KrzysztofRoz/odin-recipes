Mochi

Description:

Mochi is a bun-shaped Japanese dessert made from sweet glutinous rice flour, or mochigome. It has a soft, chewy texture that is somewhat elastic. Mochi si traditional dessert in New Years Eve in Japan, but also on Children's Day or Girl's Day. Mochi without filling also can be serve with roast or grill meat and as an addition to the soup.

  • Prep: 30 mins
  • Cook: 5 mins
  • Additional: 1 hr
  • Total: 1 hr 35 mins
  • Servings: 8
  • Yield: 8 mochi
